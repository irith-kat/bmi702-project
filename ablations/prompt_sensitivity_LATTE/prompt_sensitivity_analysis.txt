The main finding on these tests is that the interview phase is effective, but it cannot comprehensively cover every single detail, especially for LATTE as it has many possible dimensions. It proves that while the pipeline removes the technical barrier for accessing this cohort building algorithms, it still requires clinical knowledge to read and validate the protocol, and the more detail in the prompt (inclusion criteria, silver label construction, time aggregation...) the less modifications you will need to do to the protocol. The agent is very good at documenting everything that he does, so whatever is stated in the protocol he will do, and any modifications he will justify in RESULTS.md.

The conclusion is to have a clear idea of what cohort you want to build, if any design questions are unclear plan them with another LLM of choice, and once clear send a prompt to our system and watch the cohort build itself!
